How the verdict works

Each episode, two managers face the same five stocks and must commit — out loud, on camera — to a single call on each:

● BUYconviction it outperforms from here
● HOLDhappy to own it, not adding
● SELLthe risk/reward no longer stacks up

No hedging, no "it depends" — that discipline is exactly why it's become the most-watched thing Livewire makes. Every episode is also written up as a wire, so you can read the calls and revisit the reasoning any time.
